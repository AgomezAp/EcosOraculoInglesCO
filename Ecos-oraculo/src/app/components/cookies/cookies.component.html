Cookie Policy

In compliance with the provisions of the Law 34/2002, of July 11, on Information Society Services and Electronic Commerce (LSSI-CE), and the Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR), we inform users about the use of cookies on this website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They allow the site to remember your visit, your preferences, and improve your browsing experience.

What types of cookies do we use?

  • Technical cookies: necessary for the proper functioning of the website.
  • Personalization cookies: allow remembering preferences such as language or region.
  • Analytics cookies: help us measure and analyze the use of the website to improve it.
  • Behavioral advertising cookies: allow showing personalized ads based on your browsing profile.

What is the purpose of using cookies?

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the proper functioning of the website.
  • Remember your browsing preferences.
  • Analyze traffic and improve our services.
  • Offer you personalized advertising based on your browsing habits.

Consent

When you first access our website, we show you a cookie notice. In that notice you can accept all cookies, configure them or reject them. You can change your settings at any time from your browser options or from our Cookie Preferences Center (if available).

Disable or delete cookies

You can disable or delete cookies set on your device at any time through your browser options. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect the proper functioning of the website.
